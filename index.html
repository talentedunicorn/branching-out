Thank you for visiting, we'd like to present Branching out. A zine by [Artists' names]

Branching out zine is collaboration works of art and prints by artists from Malaysia, Australia and the United States.

.01

Branching out started as a zine incorporating artists within Malaysia and abroad. The idea was to produce a zine that could connect artists from different backgrounds and styles. As the process continues Branching out is expected to evolve into a multitude of other ideas.
